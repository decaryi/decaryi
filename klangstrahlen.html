Klangstrahlen

Audiovisual Soundperformance

"Klangstrahlen" is a audiovisual sound performance in which sound is visualized in an analog and direct manner. Modified CRT televisions are used to generate complex Lissajous patterns for this purpose. Both the music and the images are created live in the moment and cannot be reproduced, making the performance a unique and unforgettable experience. The combination of sound and visual art creates a unique synesthetic experience, where the senses are addressed in unconventional ways.
